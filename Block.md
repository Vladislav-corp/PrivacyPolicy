## Privacy Policy for Block

**Effective date:** April 2026

Block is an iOS puzzle game where you place tetromino-shaped pieces on an 8x8 board to clear rows and columns. Two modes: Classic (endless) and Adventure (40 levels).

### Data Collection

Block does **not collect, store, process, or share any personal data**.

The app:

* does not require user accounts or login
* does not collect names, emails, or other personal identifiers
* does not track your location
* does not access contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
* does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
* does not contain advertising or ad-related trackers
* does not transmit any data over the internet

All functionality runs locally on your device.

### Data Storage

All game data (high scores, level progress, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data is never sent to any server.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

### Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs. There are no external network requests.

### Children's Privacy

Block does not collect data from any user, including children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

### Changes

If this policy changes in the future, updates will be posted on this page.

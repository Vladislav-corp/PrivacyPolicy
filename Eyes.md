## Privacy Policy for Eyes

**Effective date:** February 2026

Eyes is a macOS application designed to help users follow the 20-20-20 rule and reduce eye strain.

### Data Collection

Eyes does **not collect, store, process, or share any personal data**.

The app:

* does not require user accounts
* does not use analytics services
* does not track usage
* does not use third-party SDKs
* does not transmit any data to external servers

All functionality runs locally on your device.

### Notifications

Eyes uses standard macOS system notifications to remind you to rest your eyes.
No notification data is stored or transmitted.

### Changes

If this policy changes in the future, updates will be posted on this page.
